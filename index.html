Shanshan Zhong (钟珊珊)

Hello there! My name is Shanshan Zhong, and I am a second-year master student from the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Sun Yat-sen University, advised by Prof. Wushao Wen and IEEE Fellow Prof. Liang Lin.
My research interests primarily revolve around multimodality, including multimodal large lanugage models, diffusion models, multimodal recommendation, and attention mechanism.

I am seeking a PhD position starting in Fall '25! Please feel free to drop me an email!
