I am a CS Ph.D. student at Carnegie Mellon University starting 2025 Fall, advised by Prof. Chenyan Xiong. Prior to this, I earned both my master's and bachelor's degrees from Sun Yat-sen University, where I was advised by IEEE Fellow Prof. Liang Lin and Prof. Wushao Wen. I have also had the pleasure of collaborating with Prof. Alan Yuille and Jieneng Chen at CCVL, as well as Prof. Pan Zhou and Shanghua Gao at Sea AI Lab. I also had a great experience contributing to OneFlow. My research interests include:

  LLM Reasoning, Memory, and Agent Optimization
  1. Low-cost long-context benchmarks [MiniLongBench] and LLM routing [RouterEval]
  2. Chain-of-thought search for optimal reasoning paths [Neural CoT Search]
  3. Creative leap-of-thought thinking for multimodal LLMs [CLoT]
  4. Causality-aware creativity evaluation for multimodal LLMs [LoTbench]
  5. Data-driven analysis of agent skills for extending LLM functionality [Agent Skills]
  Web & Information Retrieval
  1. Generative search engine optimization for web content [AutoGEO]
  2. Robust multimodal recommendation via flat local minima [Mirror Gradient]
  3. Variational information bottleneck distillation for multimodal RecSys [DVIB]
